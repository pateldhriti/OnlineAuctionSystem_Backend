About Online Auction

Online Auction started as a simple idea: buying and selling at auction shouldn't require any manual bookkeeping. Sellers list an item with a starting price and a deadline; bidders compete for the best price; when the clock runs out, the highest bid wins automatically and both sides are notified. No spreadsheets, no chasing people down for payment status, no guesswork about who won.

We're a small, focused platform - live bidding, automatic winner selection, and direct messaging between buyers and sellers, and nothing you don't need.
