About AuctionHub

AuctionHub started as a simple idea: buying and selling at auction shouldn't require any manual bookkeeping. Sellers list an item with a starting price and a deadline; bidders compete for the best price; when the clock runs out, the highest bid wins automatically and both sides are notified.

No spreadsheets, no chasing people down for payment status, no guesswork about who won.

We're a small, focused platform — live bidding, automatic winner selection, proxy auto-bidding, snipe protection, and direct messaging between buyers and sellers. Everything you need, nothing you don't.
