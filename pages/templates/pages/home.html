What is Online Auction?

A lightweight marketplace where sellers list items with a starting price and a deadline, and bidders compete for the best price. When time runs out, the highest bid wins automatically and both sides are notified — nothing to referee.

Live bidding

See the current highest bid and countdown update in real time on every listing.

Automatic winners

Auctions close themselves on schedule and pick the highest bidder — no manual work, and ties are broken fairly by whoever bid first.

Direct messaging

Bidders and sellers can message each other privately about any listing they're bidding on.
